About Us

Our Story

Book My Trip was born from a passion for travel and a vision to make global adventures more accessible. Starting in 2020 with a small team of enthusiastic travellers, we quickly became one of the fastest-growing online travel agencies serving thousands of happy customers worldwide. From humble beginnings, our focus has always been on honesty, affordability, and unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to empower people to explore the world without stress. We believe travel should be about experiences, not hidden fees or confusing processes. That’s why we focus on transparent pricing, visa support, and 24×7 WhatsApp assistance. We aim to be the most trusted companion for every traveller’s journey.

Our Vision

We envision a world where travelling is effortless and enjoyable. Whether it’s a family vacation to Singapore, a honeymoon in Bali, or a solo trip across Europe, we want to make it possible for everyone to travel confidently with our guidance.

What We Offer

Our services include:

  • Customized holiday packages with hotels, transfers, and activities.
  • Visa assistance for Dubai, Singapore, Schengen, and more.
  • Flight and hotel bookings with guaranteed best prices.
  • Instant WhatsApp and phone support from real humans, not bots.
  • Curated itineraries balancing sightseeing, relaxation, and culture.

Why Choose Us

With countless travel websites online, why pick Book My Trip? The answer lies in our customer-first approach. We provide handpicked hotels, trusted local guides, flexible bookings, and most importantly—no hidden costs. Our reviews speak for themselves: thousands of travellers rate us highly for transparency and support.
