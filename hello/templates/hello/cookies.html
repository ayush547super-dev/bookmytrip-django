Cookies Policy

Understand how we use cookies to enhance your experience.

This Cookies Policy explains how Book My Trip uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve performance, and provide a better user experience.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies – Required for basic website functionality, such as navigation and security.
  • Performance Cookies – Help us understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
  • Functional Cookies – Remember your preferences, like language and region.
  • Advertising Cookies – Used to deliver relevant ads and measure their effectiveness.

3. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies to make our website easier to use, personalize content, analyze traffic, and provide targeted advertising. Cookies help us offer you a seamless booking experience.

4. Managing Your Cookies

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain features of our website, including the ability to book or enquire about packages.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by trusted third-party services we use, such as Google Analytics or advertising partners. These help us measure performance and deliver relevant promotions.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookies Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.
