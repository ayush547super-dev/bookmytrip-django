Help Center

Search common questions or browse by topic. Prefer WhatsApp? We usually reply within 5–30 minutes during support hours.

Booking & Enquiries

How do I book a package?
Go to Packages, choose an option, and hit Enquire. We’ll confirm dates, inclusions, and payment on WhatsApp or email.
Can I customize my itinerary?
Yes. Tell us your preferred travel dates, budget, hotel type, and activities. We’ll tailor the plan and share a revised quote.
Do you arrange flights?
We can include flights on request or share best options for you to book directly—your choice.

Payments & Invoices

What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, cards, and bank transfer for India. International cards and wires available on request.
Will I receive an invoice and booking voucher?
Yes. We send a tax invoice and booking voucher to your email/WhatsApp after payment.
Is the price final? Any hidden fees?
No hidden fees. Your final quote includes all listed inclusions and taxes unless specified.

Visa & Documents

Do you assist with visas?
Yes—Dubai, Singapore, Schengen and more. We provide document checklists, forms, and appointment guidance.
What documents are usually required?
  • Passport (valid 6+ months from travel date)
  • Photos (as per destination specs)
  • Bank statements / ITR (where applicable)
  • Travel plan: flights, hotel, itinerary
We’ll share a destination-specific checklist during enquiry.
How long does visa processing take?
Varies by country and season—usually 3–21 working days after biometrics/appointment.

Changes, Cancellations & Refunds

Can I change my travel dates after booking?
Yes, subject to availability and partner policies. Fare differences and change fees may apply.
What is the cancellation policy?
Policies vary by airline/hotel/vendor. We’ll share exact terms before payment. Refunds, if applicable, are processed to your original payment method.
How long do refunds take?
Typically 5–10 business days after approval; international refunds may take longer due to bank processing.

Website Help & Troubleshooting

I can’t see images or sections on the site.
Try a hard refresh (Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R), clear cache, or open in a new browser/incognito. If it persists, share a screenshot and device info on WhatsApp.
I didn’t receive a confirmation email.
Check spam/junk and add our address to your contacts. If still missing, ping us on WhatsApp—we’ll resend.
